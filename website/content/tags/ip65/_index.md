**IP65** is an IEC 60529 ingress-protection rating with two digits. The first digit, **6**, is the highest solids rating: the enclosure is *dust-tight*, with complete protection against dust ingress. The second digit, **5**, means the device is protected against *low-pressure water jets* projected from a 6.3 mm nozzle from any direction.

This combination suits demanding environments where particulates and splashing or spraying water are present:

- Outdoor installations exposed to rain, wind-blown dust, and dirt
- Industrial floors and equipment subject to washdown or hose cleaning
- Agricultural, parking, and street-level deployments

IP65 is *not* rated for immersion. It resists jets but not submersion, so it is not a substitute for IP67 or IP68 where temporary or continuous underwater operation is required.

Enclosure rating matters when selecting a LoRaWAN device because it determines where the hardware can survive long term. Matching the IP rating to the real environment, rather than over- or under-specifying, balances durability, cost, and form factor.
