**IP51** is an IEC 60529 ingress-protection rating. The first digit, **5**, means the enclosure is *dust-protected*: a limited amount of dust may enter, but not enough to interfere with operation. It is not fully dust-tight. The second digit, **1**, means the device is protected against **vertically falling water drops** only, such as condensation or light dripping from above.

This makes IP51 a modest, indoor-oriented rating. It suits sheltered locations where some airborne dust is present and overhead drips may occur, but it is not built for splashing, spraying, washdown, or outdoor rain.

When choosing a LoRaWAN device, the enclosure rating determines where you can safely mount it:

- **IP51 is appropriate for:** indoor utility spaces, ceilings, and protected equipment rooms.
- **Look higher for:** dusty or wet sites (IP54+), washdown areas, or any outdoor placement.

Match the rating to the real installation environment so dust and moisture do not shorten the device's service life.
