JENG IoT

JENG IoT is a Dutch based company providing sensor devices and e2e IoT solutions. The devices communicate over LoRaWAN® and have an ultra-low power consumption enables operating for several years on batteries. In our cloud environment, powered by our partner Yodiwo, our devices and solutions are available off-the-shelf. We offer great dashboard apps and easy integration into your own IT environment. Applications areas are smart cities & tracking devices (containers).
