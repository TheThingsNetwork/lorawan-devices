LoRaWAN® Solar-GPS-Tracker

By dnt Innovation GmbH

The dnt LoRaWAN® Asset Tracker Solar is a reliable and compact GNSS tracker with a water- and dust proof housing according to IP44. Through the built-in solar cell, self-sufficient operation is possible. The position determination can be done cyclically or motion-based. In addition, there are many configuration options through LoRaWAN® downlinks, like the motion sensitivity or the positioning interval. The average sleep mode current is below 10 µA.
