Lynx - Micro LoRa GPS Tracker

By Rivercity Innovations Ltd.

The Lynx is a micro LoRa GPS tracker meant for use on small assets such as bikes and pets. Charging is made easy with a built in micro-usb charging port and an LED status indicator. Product of BeeSecure by Rivercity Innovations Ltd.
