Motion Sensor

By Browan Communication Incorp.

The Browan Motion Sensor integrates dual passive infrared detectors and the fresnel lens provides a 7m detection range. It utilizes LoRaWAN® connectivity to transmit data and is suitable for facility management systems to optimize facility usage or cleanliness for desks, conference rooms, bathrooms, etc.
