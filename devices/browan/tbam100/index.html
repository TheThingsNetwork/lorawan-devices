Ambient Light Sensor

By Browan Communication Incorp.

The Browan Ambient Light Sensor integrates an optical sensor providing measurements of ambient light intensity which matches the human eye’s response to light under a variety of lighting conditions. It utilizes LoRaWAN® connectivity to transmit data.
