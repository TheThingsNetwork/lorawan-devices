About this device

Digi XBee LR LoRaWAN module (MMT with U.FL antenna connector). Software-defined radio with multi-region support and X-ON zero-touch provisioning. Use XBee Studio for configuring your lorawan settings.

The XBee-LR supports 2 regional frequency plans and operates as a Class A/B/C LoRaWAN device.

Key features

  • 2 frequency plans supported: EU863-870, US902-928.
  • Battery powered.
