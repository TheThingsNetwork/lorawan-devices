S2104 - LoRaWAN® Soil Moisture and Temperature Sensor

By SenseCAP

SenseCAP S2104 - LoRaWAN® Soil Temperature and Moisture sensors satisfy industrial long-distance data acquisition with a wide range of 0 ~ 100% (m³/m³) and -40 ~ 80℃ respectively. With Bluetooth 5.0 for easy configuration and firmware upgrade and a built-in replaceable battery for minimal maintenance, it also supports three different LoRaWAN® network architectures enabling you to easily join it in existing gateways and network servers to build flexible and high-performance sensor network.
