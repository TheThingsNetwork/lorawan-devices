Wireless CO2 Sensor - LoRaWAN®

By SenseCAP

The Seeed Studio SenseCAP Wireless CO2 Sensor connects an external NDIR CO2 sensor that measures levels of CO2 emission in the atmosphere. It's dustproof, optimal for use cases that need reliable data collection, even in dusted indoor environments such as chicken farms. The collected data is transferred via LoRaWAN®.
