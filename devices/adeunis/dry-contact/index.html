Dry Contact - I/O Interface

By Adeunis

The Adeunis Dry Contact has 4 configurable inputs/outputs for connecting sensors (E.g. contact sensor, double level sensor, water leak spot, or water leak cable) and provides LoRaWAN® connectivity. It reports 0-1 states and controls relays.
