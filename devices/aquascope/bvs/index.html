Ball Valve Servo BVSLWE01

By Aqua-Scope Technologies

The Aqua-Scope Ball Valve Servo consists of a patented motor coupling that fits with existing ball valves and converts them into smart water control devices. It can receive commands directly from a flood sensor, aqua-scope controller, or LoRaWAN® gateways.
