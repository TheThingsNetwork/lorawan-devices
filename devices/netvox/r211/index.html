R211 - Wireless IR Blaster

By Netvox Technology Co., Ltd

Netvox Wireless IR Blaster can be used for IR learning and application. After IR learning, R211 can send the infrared control signal to corresponded electric devices. R211 can receive infrared signal from other infrared device and process predefined action, ex. controlling other LoRa devices.
