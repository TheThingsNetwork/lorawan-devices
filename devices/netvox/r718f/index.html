Wireless Reed Switch Open/Close Detection Sensor

By Netvox Technology Co., Ltd

The Netvox R718F - Wireless Reed Switch Open/Close Detection Sensor is a LoRaWAN® end device that has a reed switch to detect whether two objects are separated or not. Ideal for detecting the status of a door or window for security purposes.
