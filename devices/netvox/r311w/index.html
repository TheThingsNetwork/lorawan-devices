R311W - Wireless Water leak Sensor

By Netvox Technology Co., Ltd

The Netvox R311W Water Leak Detector comes with two water leak sensors suitable for detecting water and other liquid leaks in residential and commercial buildings. When the sensor detects a leak, an alarm message will be sent to the LoRaWAN® network.
