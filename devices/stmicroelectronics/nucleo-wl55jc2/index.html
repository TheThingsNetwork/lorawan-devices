NUCLEO-WL55JC2

By STMicroelectronics International NV

The STMicroelectronics NUCLEO-WL55JC2 is a development board based on the STM32WL55JC microcontroller that comes with a built-in temperature sensor and a light sensor. The NUCLEO-WL55JC2 board supports a wide choice of Integrated Development Environments (IDE) and an expansion connector for Arduino UNO V3. Suitable for rapid prototyping of LoRaWAN® end nodes by adding sensors and actuators.
