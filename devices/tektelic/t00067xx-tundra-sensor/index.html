T00067xx - TUNDRA Temperature Sensor

By Tektelic Communications Inc.

TEKTELIC’s TUNDRA Temperature Sensor is the ideal solution for maintaining optimal and consistent temperatures within cold rooms and refrigerated areas. The device can be implemented within cold storage such as fridges, coolers, cold rooms and even freezers with minimal impact on battery life or radio signal strength. The Cold Room Temperature Sensor is seamlessly integrated with TEKTELIC’s end-to-end IoT solution.
