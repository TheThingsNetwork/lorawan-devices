T0007377 - PELICAN Enterprise Outdoor Tracker

By Tektelic Communications Inc.

TEKTELIC PELICAN [Enterprise Outdoor Tracker] is an ideal solution for locating, tracking and managing assets in difficult RF environments such as manufacturing, shipping and rail yards and equipment storage facilities. This device combines the long range, low power benefits of LoRaWAN® with the universal availability and reliability of Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE).
