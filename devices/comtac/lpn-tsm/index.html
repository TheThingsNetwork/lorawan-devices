LPN TSM Transformer station monitor

By Comtac AG

The Comtac LPN TSM Transformer station monitor records most of the relevant metering values and signals for the monitoring of the operating status and the transmission of error and alarm messages. It also includes a temperature sensor for room temperature monitoring.
