About this device

The Comtac LPN TSM Transformer station monitor records most of the relevant metering values and signals for the monitoring of the operating status and the transmission of error and alarm messages. It also includes a temperature sensor for room temperature monitoring.

The LPN TSM Transformer station monitor supports 1 regional frequency plan and operates as a Class A/C LoRaWAN device.

Key features

  • Sensors: temperature, battery, voltage, current, digital input.
  • LoRa Alliance certified, verified interoperability with The Things Stack and other LoRaWAN network servers.
  • 1 frequency plan supported: EU863-870.
  • Battery powered (LiMn02 5Ah Backup battery), user replaceable.
