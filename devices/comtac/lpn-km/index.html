LPN KM - Short-circuit monitor

By Comtac AG

The Comtac LPN KM Short Circuit monitor detects short-circuit and ground fault. The integrated battery-supported power supply and the easy integration into private or public LoRaWAN® networks allow a cost-effective upgrade of stations for faster fault localization and resupply.
