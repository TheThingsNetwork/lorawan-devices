About this device

The mfc88 MCF-LW12TERWP is a LoRaWAN® end device that has a temperature sensor, humidity sensor, and pressure sensor for monitoring outdoor environments. Ideally suited for a wide range of applications such as weather stations, urban monitoring, air quality, industrial, environmental, or farming projects.

The MCF-LW12TERWP - Outdoor Environmental Sensor supports 4 regional frequency plans and operates as a Class A LoRaWAN device. A payload codec is published in the repository, so uplinks are decoded automatically when you onboard it to The Things Stack.

Key features

  • Sensors: temperature, humidity, barometer, battery.
  • LoRa Alliance certified, verified interoperability with The Things Stack and other LoRaWAN network servers.
  • Payload codec included, uplink decoder ready for one-click activation.
  • 4 frequency plans supported: AS923, AU915-928, EU863-870, US902-928.
  • Battery powered (pack), user replaceable.
