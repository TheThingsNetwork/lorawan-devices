Optical Distance Monitor

By IoTsens Gimeno IoT Division

The IoTsens LoRaWAN® Optical Distance Monitor is equipped with a LIDAR sensor for monitoring filling levels. Thus, it becomes an optimal tool in waste management, and can also be used in the detection of liquid levels in tanks, among other applications.
