EMB-LR1280-mPCI-4x

By Embit SRL

The embit EMB-LR1280-mPCIe-4x is a mini PCI express multichannel LoRa® card. It can operate worldwide being fully compliant with 2.4 GHz frequency band regulation. It is suitable for applications that require long-range connectivity like logistic and supply chain, industrial monitoring and control, factory automation, home & building automation, and wireless alarm & security systems.
