MClimate Air Quality Sensor & Notifier (AQI)

By MClimate

The MClimate AQI Sensor and Notifier is a LoRaWAN® indoor Air Quality Index Sensor and Notifier featuring gas, humidity, pressure, and temperature sensors. These sensors measure AQI, VOC (ppm), temperature, humidity, and barometric pressure. It also has a configurable buzzer and RGB LED to notify tenants if the Air Quality Index is below a configurable threshold.
