Remote Water Meter Reader XL

By Develiot

The Develiot Remote Water Meter Reader XL comes with 2 digital inputs, 2 analog inputs, 2 digital outputs and with an embedded temperature sensor. It is designed to easily connect with standard pulse water meters or other 3rd party equipment and it transmits data wirelessly and autonomously utilizing LoRaWAN® network protocol.
