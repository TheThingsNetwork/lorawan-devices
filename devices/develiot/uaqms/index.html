About this device

The Develiot Urban Air Quality Monitoring Station equipped with a wide range of sensors such as air quality (NO2, SO2, O3, CO, NH3, H2S, PH3, Cl2, HCl, HF, O2, CH2O, C2H4, C6H6, H2, PM10, PM2.5, PM1.0), CO2, noise level, temperature, humidity, pressure, wind speed, wind direction, and precipitation. It provides GSM, NBIoT, and LoRaWAN wireless connectivity for sending data.

The Urban Air Quality Monitoring Station supports 9 regional frequency plans and operates as a Class A LoRaWAN device.

Key features

  • Sensors: altitude, barometer, battery, co2, gps, temperature, humidity, wind direction, wind speed, sound.
  • LoRa Alliance certified, verified interoperability with The Things Stack and other LoRaWAN network servers.
  • 9 frequency plans supported: AS923, AU915-928, CN470-510, CN779-787, EU863-870, IN865-867, KR920-923, RU864-870, US902-928.
  • Battery powered (Lead Acid), user replaceable.
