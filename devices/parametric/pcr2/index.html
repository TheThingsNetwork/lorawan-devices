PCR2 Radar People Counter

By Parametric Engineering GmbH

Parametric‘s PCR2 people counter uses radar signals to provide a camera-less solution. Utilising LoRaWAN™ technology, these sensors transmit data at regular intervals. With bi-directional counting, the Parametric PCR2 people counter registers people passing by from left or right.
