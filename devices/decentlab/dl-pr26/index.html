DL-PR26 - Pressure / Liquid Level and Temperature Sensor

By Decentlab GmbH

The Decentlab DL-PR26 consists of a pressure/liquid level sensor and a temperature sensor. It is made for measuring and monitoring liquid levels and pressure in tanks, wells, groundwater, boreholes, and also leak detection, and pump monitoring.
