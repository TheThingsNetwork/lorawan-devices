About this device

The YOBIIQ iQ Opentherm Gateway Boiler Controller, part of our Smart Environmental Control Modules, seamlessly integrates practical usability with advanced LoRaWAN technology. Consequently, this Opentherm Controller facilitates smart boiler controls, making it a key component of Smart Boiler Solutions. By reading real-time data from your connected thermostat and boiler, it allows you to either monitor or actively manage your boiler’s operation. As a result, it becomes an essential element of Smart Building Solutions, enhancing overall Environmental Control.

The iQ Opentherm - LoRaWAN Boiler Controller supports 1 regional frequency plan and operates as a Class A/C LoRaWAN device. A payload codec is published in the repository, so uplinks are decoded automatically when you onboard it to The Things Stack.

Key features

  • Sensors: temperature, humidity, snr, rssi, digital input.
  • Payload codec included, uplink decoder ready for one-click activation.
  • 1 frequency plan supported: EU863-870.
