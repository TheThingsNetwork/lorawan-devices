About this device

The Senzemo Senpuck Pure is an indoor LoRaWAN® air quality sensor that measures CO2, TVOC, temperature, realtive humidity, and pressure. Suitable for monitoring air quality in enterprise and public spaces. The device sends data to the LoRaWAN® network and notifies users to open the windows if the air quality is poor.

The SPU20B - Senspuck Pure Battery supports 4 regional frequency plans and operates as a Class A LoRaWAN device. A payload codec is published in the repository, so uplinks are decoded automatically when you onboard it to The Things Stack.

Key features

  • Sensors: co2, tvoc, temperature, humidity, pressure.
  • Payload codec included, uplink decoder ready for one-click activation.
  • 4 frequency plans supported: AS923, AU915-928, EU863-870, US902-928.
  • Battery powered (ER14505), user replaceable.

Manual setup

In The Things Stack console, go to Applications → Create application and pick a unique application ID.

Choose From the LoRaWAN Device Repository and search for “SPU20B - Senspuck Pure Battery”. Select your firmware version and frequency plan.

3
Enter device credentials

Provide the DevEUI, JoinEUI and AppKey printed on the device label or QR code.

4
Power on & verify uplinks

Within a few minutes you'll see the join request and decoded uplinks in the Live data tab.
