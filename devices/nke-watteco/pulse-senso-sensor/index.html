Pulse Sens'O - Smart Meter Interface

By nke WATTECO

The nke WATTECO Pulse Sens’O consists of a pulse sensor (counter) for remotely reading metering data from three meters - water, gas, electricity, or energy. It turns existing meters into smart meters via a public or private LoRaWAN® network.
