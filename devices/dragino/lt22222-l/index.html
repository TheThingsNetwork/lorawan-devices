LT22222-L - I/O Controller

By Dragino Technology Co., Limited

The Dragino LT22222-L is a LoRaWAN® I/O module that contains different I/O Interfaces such as analog current input, analog voltage input, relay output, digital input, and digital output. The LT series I/O modules are designed to simplify the installation of I/O monitoring.
