Vaqa'O Multi Lite - Temperature, Humidity, Luminosity, CO2 & motion sensor

By WATTECO

The Watteco VAQA’O Multi Lite is a LoRaWAN® device that measures Temp/Hum/CO2/Lum/Motion Sensor in the ambient air of a room, in a building, an apartment, or a house. The data is transmitted via a public or private LoRaWAN® radio frequency network.
