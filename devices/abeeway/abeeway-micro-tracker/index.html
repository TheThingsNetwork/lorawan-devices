Micro Tracker

By Abeeway

The Abeeway Micro Tracker is a multi-mode tracker for indoor and outdoor use. It provides accurate indoor and outdoor positioning using embedded geolocation sensors combining GPS, Low-power GPS, Wi-Fi Sniffing, BLE, and LoRaWAN® TDoA geolocation technologies. Ideal for tracking anything including people, pets, vehicles, and assets with antitheft and geofencing technologies.
