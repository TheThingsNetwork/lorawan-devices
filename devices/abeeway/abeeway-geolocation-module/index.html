Geolocation Module

By Abeeway

The Abeeway Geolocation module for outdoor geolocation contains a high-performance multi-constellation GNSS receiver (GPS, GLONASS, Beidou, and Galileo) and supports a patented ultra Low-Power GPS (LP GPS) mode. Indoor positioning can be achieved by combining the Bluetooth Low Energy receiver and the ultra-low power Wi-Fi receiver.
