DF703 - Ultrasonic Waste Bin Level Sensor

By Dingtek

The CNDingtek df703 is an ultrasonic level waste bin sensor that consists of a level sensor, fire sensor, and accelerometer. The measured data is reported to a LoRaWAN® network that notifies garbage collecting authorities to collect and empty the bins.
