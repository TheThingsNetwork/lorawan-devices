LoRa Service Call Button

By Mutelcor GmbH

The Mutelcor LoRa Service Call Button is a LoRaWAN® Device that raises alarms or requests to multiple services for help, assistance or support from a single device in real time in a matter of seconds by pressing a button. A physical feedback to the button press using a buzzer and LED can be configured.
