ERS smart building sensor

By Elsys

The ELSYS ERS is a LoRaWAN® device that consists of motion, temperature, humidity, and light sensors for measuring occupancy and the indoor environment. It is suitable for applications such as indoor environment measuring, smart buildings, workplace management, workplace statistics, facility management, and room occupancy.
