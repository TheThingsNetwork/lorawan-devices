ERS Lite indoor environment monitoring

By Elsys

The ELSYS ERS Lite is an indoor LoRaWAN® room sensor that contains two internal sensors for measuring indoor temperature and humidity. It is suitable for applications such as indoor environment measuring, smart buildings, and workplace management.
