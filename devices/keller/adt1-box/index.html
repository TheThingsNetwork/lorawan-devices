ADT1 Box - Remote Transmitter

By KELLER Druckmesstechnik AG

The KELLER ADT1-Box is an autonomous device that can be linked with digital level probes and pressure transmitters in the KELLER D and X product lines. The measured data can be collected through a LoRaWAN® network.
